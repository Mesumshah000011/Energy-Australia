How to Contact Energy Australia – Phone, Email & Live Chat

Need to speak to someone at Energy Australia? Whether you're moving house, sorting out a bill, or just have a question about your plan, getting in touch is pretty easy. This guide breaks down all the ways you can reach out—phone, email, chat, and a few handy online tools too.

How You Can Reach Energy Australia

Call Them

If you're more comfortable talking on the phone, use the energy australia contact number to speak directly with their support team. They can help with billing issues, plan changes, or anything else related to your account or service.

Need Help Fast?

  • Call for emergencies like fire or injury.
  • For urgent energy problems, get in touch with your local energy distributor. They're the ones who handle wires, pipes, and outages.

Email Support

Energy Australia doesn't offer a general email address, but you can still reach them online. Use their contact form or the live chat feature on the website to start a conversation.

For Billing Questions:
If you need help with invoices or payments, fill out the online form to have someone from the billing team get in touch.

Use Live Chat

Don't feel like waiting on the phone? You can speak to someone via live chat on their website. Head to their Contact Us page and click the chat option to get started.

Getting a New Electricity or Gas Set Up

Setting Up Electricity at a New Place

If you're moving into a new home and need an energy australia connection , here's how to get it done:

  • Visit the Energy Australia website and go to the "Moving House" section.
  • Enter your new address, your move-in date, and your personal details.
  • Choose a plan that fits your lifestyle. There are solar options too.
  • Review everything and submit your request. Energy Australia will handle the rest.

Timing Tip:

Book at least two business days ahead of your move to ensure a smooth setup.

Moving Day Promise:

If your Energy Australia connection isn't ready when you move in and you gave enough notice, you could receive a $100 credit on your first bill. (Conditions apply.)

Need Gas Too?

The gas connection process is similar:

  • Use the same "Moving House" section online.
  • Enter your details and preferred connection date.
  • Choose a gas plan based on your usage.
  • Submit the form to schedule the setup.

How Long It Takes:

Allow at least two business days for gas connections.

Moving House with an Existing Account

Already an Energy Australia customer? You can take your energy with you:

  • Log in to My Account.
  • Click the "Moving House" tab.
  • Enter your new address and move-in date.
  • Confirm your details and submit the request.

A Few Tips:

  • Book the move at least three business days ahead.
  • If your new place has solar panels, call Energy Australia contact support to make sure everything is connected correctly.

What to Do If There's a Problem?

Delay in Connection:
If your electricity or gas isn't connected on time, reach out as soon as possible so they can investigate and resolve the issue.

Using Online Tools to Manage Your Energy

My Account

The My Account portal is your go-to spot online. You can:

  • View and pay bills
  • Track energy usage
  • Update contact and bank details
  • Set up direct debit
  • Download past bills
  • Notify them about a move

Just register or log in via the Energy Australia website.

Use Their App

Prefer not to log in every time? Download the Energy Australia app for iOS or Android. You'll be able to:

  • Make payments
  • View bills and usage
  • Track your energy habits
  • Receive updates and reminders

Try the Moving Tool Online

The online Moving House tool makes the Energy Australia connection process quick and simple:

  • Submit your new address
  • Set the date for your connection
  • Select a suitable plan
  • Complete the move without having to call

It's available 24/7 and works on mobile too.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up a new electricity connection?

Go to the Energy Australia site, enter your address and move-in date, pick a plan, and submit the form.

How do I get gas connected?

The process is similar to electricity—enter your details and plan choice in the Moving House section.

Can I move my energy to a new address?

Yes. Use the "Moving House" tab in My Account. Give at least three business days' notice.

How long does it take to connect?

Electricity typically takes two business days; gas may take up to three.

What if there's no power on move-in day?

Call the moving team. If you gave notice and the setup failed, you may get a $100 bill credit.

Can I handle everything online?

Absolutely. From usage tracking to plan changes, you can manage everything through My Account or the app.

Wrapping It Up

Getting in touch with Energy Australia is straightforward. Whether you need to make an Energy Australia connection for a new home or simply have a question, you can call, chat, or message them using a method that suits you. With tools available online, you can manage your energy needs with minimal fuss. Planning ahead is the key—especially if you're moving house—so everything's ready when you need it.
